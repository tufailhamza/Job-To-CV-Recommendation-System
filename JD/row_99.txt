In this role, a successful Human Resources/Recruiting Intern will learn the inner workings of our human resources department. We are searching for a well-rounded individual who has a familiarity with human resources, as well as possesses advanced skills in Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook.  The ideal candidate will be personable, computer-savvy, communicative, and a go-getter—these will be valuable skills as the intern joins a team of growing talented employees in various states, keeping the many HR functions successful. In addition, the intern will be responsible for working on special projects selected by the department. With initiative and will, this role will lay a solid foundation for the intern’s future career path.
